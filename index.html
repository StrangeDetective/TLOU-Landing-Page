The Last Of Us

Dive into the gripping world of "The Last of Us," an award-winning action-adventure game series developed by Naughty Dog. Set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a mysterious infection, this series follows the harrowing journey of complex characters fighting for survival.

Main Characters

A rugged survivor navigating a post-apocalyptic world, haunted by personal losses yet driven by a fierce determination to protect Ellie at all costs.

A determined young survivor forging her path through a harsh post-apocalyptic world, guided by her bond with Joel and fueled by her own resilience and bravery.

A resilient and resourceful survivor who allies with Joel, bringing a blend of toughness and loyalty to their challenging journey.

A determined leader and maternal figure in the post-apocalyptic world, guiding Ellie and others with wisdom and resolve in their struggle for survival.

A seasoned survivor with a troubled past, navigating the harsh realities of a post-apocalyptic world with pragmatism and a hardened resolve.

A resourceful and eccentric survivor who reluctantly aids Joel and Ellie, navigating the post-apocalyptic world with a mix of toughness and hidden empathy.

A brave and hopeful young survivor, navigating the harsh world alongside his brother, finding moments of joy amidst the struggle.

A protective and determined older brother, navigating the dangers of a post-apocalyptic world while caring deeply for Sam, showcasing resilience and sacrifice.

A loyal and resourceful brother, striving for a better future while balancing hope and survival in a harsh world.

A spirited and adventurous teen, whose friendship with Ellie provides moments of warmth and growth amidst the chaos.
